About Me

Hi everyone, welcome to my portofolio website. My name is Rizal Fahri Irawna, born on 19 January 2004 in Wonosobo. I am an active, hard-working, and enthusiastic individual in the field of information technology. I possess the ability to work both in a team and independently, and I always strive to complete tasks with quality and in a timely manner. I also have a good command of the English language. I hope to join a company that prioritizes innovation and technology development, where I can contribute and expand my knowledge and experience.

I recently graduated from a technical high school majoring in Software Engineering in 2022 with satisfactory grades. During my high school years, I learned various technologies and programming languages, such as Java and C++. I also participated in extracurricular activities related to information technology, such as programming contests and the IT club. Currently, I am in my second semester of my computer science degree program at PGRI University Yogyakarta. Here, I deepen my knowledge and find solutions to problems in technology.

As part of my education program, I completed a 6-month on-the-job training (OJT) at BLK Kebumen. I was placed on the software development team and had the opportunity to learn and apply the knowledge I gained during my high school training. I understand how a company manages and develops software and helped the team complete various tasks. During my OJT, I also had the opportunity to work with the team to solve technical problems and ensure that the quality of the application meets industry standards. I am confident that this experience will be greatly beneficial for my career in the information technology field.

About Me

Hi everyone, welcome to my portofolio website. My name is Rizal Fahri Irawna, born on 19 January 2004 in Wonosobo. I hope to join a company that prioritizes innovation and technology development, where I can contribute and expand my knowledge and experience.

I am accustomed to handling content updates, managing website features, and ensuring system functionality and performance. I am detail-oriented, responsible, and collaborative, with the ability to work effectively both independently and within a team.

I am skilled at translating ideas and information into visually engaging designs that align with branding and communication goals. I am seeking a role where I can contribute to website infrastructure management, ongoing maintenance, and UI/UX implementation, while also leveraging my graphic design skills to ensure systems are stable, secure, visually appealing, and optimized for user engagement.
